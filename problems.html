Hercules and the Hydra

According to Greek myth, the hydra was a serpentine monster that could regrow two heads for every one cut off. Hercules, however, has been given a gigantic sword by the gods capable of cutting off all of the hydra's heads at once. If the hydra started with \(8\) heads and now has \(256\), how many times has Hercules used his sword? Express your answer as a logarithm.

\[\log_2\left(\frac{m}{n}\right)=\log_2\left(\frac{n\cdot2^x}{n}\right)=\log_2\left(2^x\right)=x\]
